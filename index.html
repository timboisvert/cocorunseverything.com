Our Tools

CocoScout

CocoScout is the all-in-one platform where performers and production teams connect. From auditions to opening night, CocoScout streamlines casting, scheduling, and communication—replacing the spreadsheets, group texts, and email chains that slow productions down. Performers build professional profiles and submit to auditions with one click, while production teams manage their entire show from a single dashboard.

For Performers:

  • Build a professional profile with headshots, resume, and media clips
  • Discover and submit to auditions with one click
  • Sync rehearsals and performances to your calendar
  • Track your audition history and upcoming calls
  • Connect with cast mates and celebrate milestones
  • Free for performers

For Production Teams:

  • Create custom audition forms with video submissions
  • Drag-and-drop casting with instant conflict visibility
  • Smart scheduling with automatic cast notifications
  • Team communications that reach the right people
  • Sign-up forms, questionnaires, and directory management
  • Performer payouts built right in
